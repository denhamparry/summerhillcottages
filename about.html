About Us

Our Story

Tina and Arfon

Summerhill Farm and Cottages ‘found us’ more than fifteen years ago during which time we have turned an idea and a dream into a reality.

The background of this beautiful collection of cottages provided us with a great starting point as does its position on the landscape.

Perfectly situated to explore coast and country and all the delights that Pembrokeshire has to offer.

We have added bits here, updated bits there, always with the aim of being able to offer our guests character, modern comforts and quality in lovely surroundings.

We work hard to offer an experience that we would love to have for ourselves, making guest satisfaction our priority.

We have met numerous lovely people over the years and made many good friends. We’d be thrilled to welcome you too.
