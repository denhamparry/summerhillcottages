The Crest

Ground floor

Summerhill Crest for 6 plus 2 toddlers (all on one level).

Bedrooms

Master bedroom with en suite and king sized bed, small TV. Second bedroom with an option for 6ft super king sized bed or twin singles. Third bedroom with twin singles.

Bathrooms

Family bathroom with bath and shower and a separate cloakroom with toilet.

Lounge

Large lounge with flat screen HDMI television, DVD player, DAB radio and wifi. French doors opening onto the terrace with open views.

Kitchen and Dining

Well equipped kitchen comprising oven and hob, fridge, dishwasher and microwave leading into dining room, both room's have lovely views across countryside to the sea.

Utility Room

Utility room with rear access to the property housing fridge freezer and washing machine/tumble dryer.

Garden

Enclosed gardens with private hottub, barbeque and ample parking.
